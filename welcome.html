Our Services

Aircon Repair

Repair or Replacement: After determining the root cause of the problem, the technician will recommend a suitable solution. Depending on the issue, they may repair or replace faulty components such as motors, capacitors, thermostats, or refrigerant lines. In some cases, the entire unit might need to be replaced if it is old or beyond repair.

System Cleaning

Along with repairs, the technician may clean the air conditioning system to remove dust, debris, or mold that can affect its performance. This may involve cleaning or replacing air filters, clearing clogged drain lines, or cleaning the evaporator and condenser coils.

Maintenance

To prevent future issues, the technician may provide maintenance recommendations. This can include regular filter replacements, coil cleanings, lubrication of moving parts, and scheduled professional maintenance to keep the air conditioner in optimal condition.
