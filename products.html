Evaporator

Evaporators are an essential component of refrigeration and air conditioning systems. They absorb heat from the surrounding environment, causing the refrigerant (typically a liquid) to evaporate and turn into a gas.

Condenser

The condenser plays a crucial role in removing heat from the refrigerant gas, causing it to condense into a liquid. The cooling medium, typically air or water, absorbs the heat from the refrigerant in the condenser, allowing it to release heat and transition to a liquid state.

Compressor

Compressor is responsible for compressing the refrigerant gas, raising its pressure and temperature. By compressing the refrigerant, the compressor enables the transfer of heat from the cooled space to the outside environment in a refrigeration system, or the compression and circulation of refrigerant in an air conditioning system.

Blower Motor

Blower Motor is an electric motor that drives a fan or impeller to circulate air or other gases. It is commonly used in various heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems and applications where air movement is required.

Fliter Drier

A filter drier, also known as a refrigerant filter drier or simply drier, is a component used in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to remove moisture, contaminants, and impurities from the refrigerant.

Expansion Valve

An expansion valve, also known as a metering device, is a critical component in refrigeration and air conditioning systems.
